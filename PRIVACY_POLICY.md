# W-SafeRoutes Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

W-SafeRoutes respects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may collect and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

The app may collect information needed to provide account access and safety-route features, including:

- Name and email address
- Password authentication data
- Login and saved session information
- Selected start and destination areas
- Route requests and safety analysis inputs
- Location data when you allow location-based features
- SOS-related details when emergency features are used

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Create and manage user accounts
- Log users in and maintain sessions
- Generate safety-route suggestions
- Display maps, route analysis, and safety zones
- Support SOS and emergency contact functionality
- Maintain, secure, and improve app functionality

## Third-Party Processing

Your information may be processed by backend hosting, database, authentication, maps, routing, geocoding, and infrastructure providers that help the app function. We do not sell your personal information.

## Location Permissions

You can deny device location permission, but some route, map, and safety features may not work fully. Location information is used to support route planning, safety analysis, and emergency functionality.

## Local Session Storage

The app may save a local session on your device so you do not need to log in every time. Logging out removes the saved local session from the device.

## Data Requests

For account deletion, privacy questions, or data requests, contact the app owner or developer using the support contact listed on the app store listing.

## Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
